## Episode Summary

In October 2015, Leon Black—billionaire chairman of Apollo Global Management—wired **$19.75 million** to Jeffrey Epstein. The money was routed through shell companies with specific instructions to hide Black's name from public records.

This episode reveals:
- The smoking gun email: "avoid public disclosure of Leon name"
- The complex shell company maze used to conceal the payment
- The "dinner party from hell" with Woody Allen, Obama White House Counsel, and Jeffrey Epstein
- Questions that demand answers about what Epstein provided in exchange
